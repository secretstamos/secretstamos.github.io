My Projects - Project 2

Mega Man X Corrupted

Dates: Dates: June 2010 - 2014

Role: Character Artist

Work Done: 2D Graphic Creation, Animation

What is the project?

Mega Man X Corrupted is a fan game intended to replicate itself as a sequel of the critically acclaimed Mega Man X games of the Super Nintendo. It has been in development for the last ten years and consists of a large team of volunteers.

What I did

Mega Man X Corrupted was one of my first projects I worked on as an artist. The project lead mentored me heavily, and gave me my tasks. For this project, I mostly worked on enemy characters, sometimes from existing templates and sometimes from scratch. I built up my skills as an animator and worked heavily to match the style through the use of rigid color schemes.
