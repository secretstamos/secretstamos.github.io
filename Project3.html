My Projects - Project 3

Hikari Hook

Dates: September 2015

Role: Project Artist

Work Done: 3D Environment Creation, 3D Effect Creation, Story Writing

What is the project?

Hikari Hook is a 3D VR game utilizing both the Razor Hydra and the Occulus Rift. It is a story-based game involving a character exploring an ancient forest armed with a grapple hook. The plot is delivered through audio logs located on different platforms in the forest.

Hikari Hook was made at Osaka University in the Fall of 2015 as part of a student-collaboration project.

What I did

I was the artist for Hikari Hook, and made all of the art assets, texture assets and animations myself. As the only artist in the group of 5 members, I was expected to generate all assets by myself. In addition to handling the graphics creation, texture creation and image mapping, I also handled some code for visual and particle effects. I did so with Javascript, and also wrote the transcript for the game's story, setting and dialogue. Lastly, I was tasked with making sure the game operated at a comfortable framerate.
